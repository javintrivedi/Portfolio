About Me

Hello! I’m Javin Trivedi, a passionate and driven B.Tech student pursuing Computer Science Engineering at SRM University, Kattankulathur. With a strong foundation in web development, a love for music, and a creative flair for art, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technology. Beyond coding, I express my creativity through art. I enjoy sketching and drawing, using pencils and colors to bring my ideas to life. Whether it’s capturing the intricacies of a portrait or experimenting with abstract designs, art allows me to explore a different dimension of creativity and self-expression.

When I’m not coding or sketching, you’ll find me strumming my guitar, exploring diverse music genres, and channeling my creativity into melodies. Music and art have taught me the value of discipline, patience, and expression—qualities I bring to my work as a developer. I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and pushing the boundaries of what I can create. My goal is to leverage my skills and experiences to contribute meaningfully to the web development field and create solutions that make a difference.

Let’s connect and explore opportunities to innovate together! Whether it’s building cutting-edge web applications, discussing the latest tech trends, or sharing creative ideas, I’m always excited to collaborate and grow.

Feel free to reach out—I’d love to hear from you!

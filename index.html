About Me

Hello! I’m Javin Trivedi, a passionate Computer Science Engineering student at SRM University, Kattankulathur, driven by the vision of blending creativity with technology. Over the past few years, I’ve transformed ideas into impactful projects that reflect both innovation and execution.

I successfully developed MindVault, a secure and intelligent iOS app designed to go beyond traditional note-taking. It features time-locked thoughts, biometric authentication, disguised calculator mode, AI-powered self-reflection, emotional insights, and interactive data visualizations. Alongside this, I created the F1 AI Platform, an interactive web application offering driver management, AI-powered quizzes, live race insights, and voice command functionality. I also built an AI-Powered Shoe Fit Analyzer using computer vision and AR to measure foot size in real-time and enable virtual try-ons.

Beyond tech, I fuel my creativity through art and music—sketching detailed portraits, experimenting with abstract designs, and composing guitar melodies. These passions shape my ability to think differently, balance precision with imagination, and bring originality to every project I work on.

I thrive in collaborative and fast-paced environments—be it hackathons, open-source contributions, or innovative projects. My long-term vision is to keep pushing boundaries in AI, AR/VR, and full-stack/mobile development while creating solutions that truly make an impact.

Let’s connect and innovate together—whether it’s brainstorming ideas, building cutting-edge applications, or sharing perspectives on the future of technology.

Feel free to reach out—I’d love to hear from you!
